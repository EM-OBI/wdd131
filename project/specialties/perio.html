What connective tissue fibers are normally found in the gingiva?

Histologically, the fibers described are the dentogingival, dentoalveolar, dentoperiosteal, circular, and transseptal fibers.

What portion of the root is covered by acellular cementum? What portion of the root is covered by cellular cementum?

Acellular cementum is located in the coronal and middle portion of the root. Cellular cementum is located in the apical third of the root.
